About I Spy, You Buy!

I Spy You Buy, is a thrift shop located in Mount Airy, Philadelphia. The unique thing about this thrift shop is that the invintory is made up completly of clothes that have been found at other thrift shops from around the area. One could say I Spy You Buy is the all star of thrift shops.

I Spy You Buy, started as an outdoor pop-up store in 2013. The store three years later moved into its current location at 7151 Germantown Ave.
